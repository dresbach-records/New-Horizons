Privacy Policy

At Projeto Novos Horizonte, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your personal information when you use our services. We are committed to protecting your data and ensuring transparency in our processes.

1. Data We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, update your profile, or communicate with us. This may include:

  • Name, email address, and professional contact details.
  • Usage data, including IP addresses and device information.
  • Preference data related to communication and system settings.

2. How We Use Data

We process your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • To process transactions and send related information.
  • To send technical notices, updates, and security alerts.
